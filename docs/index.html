Arjun Krishna
I am a CS PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania advised by Prof. Dinesh Jayaraman. I am broadly interested in the field of Robot Learning.
I received my Master’s degree from Georgia Tech where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Matthew Gombolay. I obtained my bachelor’s degree in CS from IIT Madras and was introduced to RL research there by Prof. Balaraman Ravindran. I also worked as a Software Engineer in the space of RecSys and Advertisement Bidding at Indeed Japan for about 3 years before returning to academia.
